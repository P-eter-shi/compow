# ComPow - Personal-community Safety Alert App 

ComPow is an Android safety application that allows you to quickly send emergency alerts to your trusted contacts with just a double-press of your volume buttons. The app shares your real-time location and enables continuous tracking during   
emergencies.  

Features  


->Emergency Alert Trigger: Double-press volume up or volume down buttons to trigger an alarm  

->Background Trigger: Works even when your phone is locked or the app is closed  

->Real-time Location Sharing: Automatically shares your GPS location with emergency contacts  

->Live Location Tracking: Contacts can track your location in real-time during an emergency  

->SMS Alerts: Sends emergency SMS to all your saved contacts, if offline  

->Message Alerts; Sends messages through socket.io if online  

->Contact Management: Add and manage trusted emergency contacts  

->Destination Tracking: Set a destination and let contacts know when you arrive safely or when you are out of danger  

->Alert History: View logs of all triggered alerts
